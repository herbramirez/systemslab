5x5 Assignment

For 5 days continuous days I recreated past memories using multiple 3D softwares. I set contraints on the design by requiring myself to make them as close to real as I could based around a limited amount of time. From modeling physical structures, to sunrise and sunset I tried to recreate some of my favorite places/memories from living in the Bay area.

Day 1

Bay Bridge Modeling and Arnold Rendering

On the first day I did strictly 3d modeling in Maya. I 3d modeled part of the baybridge based off of google 2D images. I used the images as reference to build a similar incomplete structure of the bridge. I then used basic Arnold Materials to color and fully render.

Day 2

Sutro Tower in Maya

On day 2 I designed my commute back to SF from work. Every day for over a year I would see the sun set over Twin peaks. I 3D modeled the Sutro tower and overlayed it in front of the sunset.Every morning leaving for work and returning from work I'd see the sun over the bay.

Day 3

Twin peaks Terrain modeling and building

Day three was focused on integrating the Maya models I made into Unity. I also focused on building terrain using Unity's terrain builder tools. I referenced google maps and spent a couple of hours trying to use a paint brush to depict the hills that make up the actual area of twin peaks.

Day 4

Sunrise and Sunset

On day four I stepped away from modeling structures to learning C#. I created a basic function that makes the sun go around based on deltaTime. That way the sun is rising and setting every couple of seconds. IT gives the illusion of sunrise/sunset
