Injuries

For my form study 2D project I wanted to represent parts of the body that fracture the most when dancing. Through research I found that bellerinas legs and feet where most prone to injuries. Ballerinas are bound to deform their bodies due to the contortions they put themsevles through. I used a voronoi effector in Cinema 4D to fracture a model and represent the areas that are prone to injuries. In a hierchy starting from the bottom, I try to depict the small parts of the body that will break and tear more opposed to those that don't. I also wanted to explore with lighting to express pain behind the elegance of the ballerinas. Pitch black to highlight the dancer and her focus. The red for the pain they endure. The blue for the calmness they show.
